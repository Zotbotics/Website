ABOUT US

What makes Zotbotics unique?

Passionate

We come from many backgrounds, from Engineering to Business, Asia to the US. But all of us at Zotbotics share one thing: passion. All of us at Zotbotics love what we do, which is why we set out to create a competetive Robotics organization at UCI.

Technical

We don't just sit back and talk about robotics. We get out there and do it! We're involved in both teaching the student body industry-relevant skills, but also involved in intercollegiate competitions that show off UCI's engineering talent.

Professional

We love having fun, but as a college organization, we also believe in developing top tier products with industry-tier tools and procedures. The way we structure our club and handle projects industry, from projects to the board we strive to be a professional organization.

SIGN UP

We're still setting things up, but there are a bunch of ways to get involved in Zotbotics! Show us you're interested through the interest form.

  • Competitions
  • Face Other Universities in a fast-paced environment, tackling real-world engineering challenges. Unfortunately robotics is expensive, so fees will apply, but we're also working on ways to provide scholarships. Fill out the interest form, join our Slack or contact us for more information!
  • Workshops
  • We will meet every couple weeks for workshops, recruitment events, or just social events. These events are open to everyone regardless of club membership status! Check out our Facebook page, sign up to our newsletter, or get on our Slack to stay informed!
  • Projects
  • We know people have their own ideas that don't necessarily fit inside the competition environment, so we are working on a way for people to propose and work on projects using Zotbotics resources! These projects are approved on a case-by-case basis, but go ahead and contact us if you have an idea!

All fees pay for materials, workshop space, and club expenses! A full club budget is available at request.
